The award, announced each year on August (Dirac's birthday), was first awarded in 1985. An international committee of distinguished scientists selects the winners from list of nominated candidates. The Committee invites nominations from scientists working in the fields of theoretical physics or mathematics. The Dirac Medal of the ICTP is not awarded to Nobel Laureates, Fields Medalists, or Wolf Prize winners.
