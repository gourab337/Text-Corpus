Nevertheless, Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures is credited at the end of the film. In an interview with ''Movieline'', producer Kevin Feige stated that the Tesseract is based on the Cosmic Cube from Marvel comics. After ''Thor: The Dark World'', in an interview with he stated that it was also the Space Infinity Stone.
